Latest News

To mark the 25th anniversary of their debut album, Rammstein are releasing "Herzeleid (XXV Anniversary Edition - Remastered)" today - as a limited special edition! The CD features the original tracklist, housed in a lavish, cross-shaped digipak with a deluxe slipcase. In addition, there is a 2LP version with a slipcase containing two 180 gram heavyweight black vinyl discs with blue splatter effect. The artwork, with Praler's original photographs, was reworked for this new release by the original designer Dirk Rudolph.

Rammstein are very happy to announce the rescheduled dates for their North America Tour today! The tour will now begin on August 22, 2021 in Montreal and end on October 1, 2021 in Mexico City. All tickets for the original shows will be honored at the rescheduled dates. Ticket holders will receive an email notification from the ticketing company soon that includes information on how to request a refund if you are unable to attend the rescheduled date.
Unfortunately, the previously scheduled show in Washington, DC has been cancelled due to scheduling issues, and refunds will be available at point of purchase.
Thank you so much for your patience, and we are looking forward to seeing you next year!
